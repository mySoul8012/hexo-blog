# 关于世界

曾经有一刻感受到，这个世界是属于你们的，不是属于我的，曾经有那么一刻感受到，这个世界和我也没有任何的关系。他永远都会按照既定的规则始终运行，和我没有任何的关系。

一个被排除在外的一个人。

未来是属于别人的，也不是属于我的。整个世界和我没有任何关系。

一个空荡荡的人。

内心空荡荡的。

-- 一些关于世界的思考。


# About the World

There was once a moment when I felt that this world belongs to you, not to me. There was once a moment when I felt that this world has nothing to do with me. It will always operate according to the established rules, and has no connection to me.

A person excluded from everything.

The future belongs to others, not to me. The entire world has nothing to do with me.

An empty person.

An empty heart.

-- Some thoughts about the world.
